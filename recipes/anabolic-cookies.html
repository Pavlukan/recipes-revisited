Anabolic Cookies

Description

Cookies shouldn't be looked down upon as sugary snacks that only reckless people can eat. You can actually prepare amazing cookies without using sugar or artificial sweeteners. Moreover, such cookies are rich in various micro and macro nutrients such as zinc, magnesium and, most importantly, PROTEIN!

Ingredients

  • 200 grams of bananas
  • 80 grams of oats
  • 16 grams of 99% dark chocolate/cocoa powder
  • 100 grams of twarog/skyr/cottage cheese

Steps

  1. Break bananas with a fork till they are a mush
  2. Add oats and mix it all together
  3. Add chocolate/cocoa powder and twarog/skyr/cottage cheese
  4. Mix until it all becomes a smooth paste
  5. Cover the baking tray with parchment paper
  6. Spoon the cookies on the tray
  7. Bake for 40 minutes under 180°C (360°F)
