Anabolic Sweets

Description

There are few people on this planet who dislike the taste of good sweets. The problem is, as a rule, the sweets we buy in the supermarket are not going to make us anabolic or healthier. But no one prevents us from preparing our own dessert that is filled with protein, vitamins and healthy fats.

Ingredients

  • Skyr/greek yoghurt/cottage cheese/twarog
  • One big banana
  • Cocoa powder
  • Honey (optional)

Steps

  1. Cut the banana into small pieces (just around 2 cm in thickness)
  2. Take a deep bowl
  3. Put 2 tablespoons of skyr/greek youhurt/cottage cheese/twarog into the bowl
  4. Put 2 teaspoons of cocoa powder or cocoa
  5. Put 1 teaspoon of honey (not necessary)
  6. Mix it all together
  7. "Soak" the banana slices in our paste
  8. Put our little sweets on a plate
  9. Let them stay in the freezer for 2-3 hours
  10. Enjoy!
