Anabolic Skyr

Description

Skyr is a product many people, especially students, miss out on. However, it is an amazing food filled with protein and various micronutrients. By combining skyr with other products that are rich in carbs or fats, we can create a tasty, filling meal that will make us even more ANABOLIC!

Ingredients

  • 200 or 400 grams of skyr
  • 50 or 100 grams of peanuts or peanut butter
  • 50 grams of oats

Steps

  1. Cook the oats in whole-fat milk or water
  2. Put the skyr into a deep bowl
  3. Add the peanut butter and mix thoroughly
  4. Put the oats into your skyr and mix again
  5. Enjoy your meal!
