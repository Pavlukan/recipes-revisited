Anabolic Toast

Description

Meat, protein powder, fish, eggs can get quite expensive in certain countries. But what is pretty cheap for most of us is bread and certain dairy products, including twarog, skyr, greek yoghurt and, sometimes, cottage cheese. So it is a good option to combine such products together to create a tasty and ANABOLIC meal.

Ingredients

  • Skyr/greek yoghurt/cottage cheese/twarog
  • A couple of slices of bread
  • Peanut Butter (optional)

Steps

  1. Put the slices into a toaster or fry them up in a pan
  2. Take them out and put on a plate
  3. Put equal amount of twarog/greek yoghurt/cottage cheese/skyr on the slices
  4. Add some peanut butter (optional)
  5. Enjoy :)
